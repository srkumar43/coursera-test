Test

Test matches are the longest form of cricket. They are played over 5 days. White jerseys are worn for playing test matches. Each team plays two innings. Every day 90 overs are bowled. There is no restriction of how many overs a bowler can bowl. The highest score by a batsman in a test match is scored by Brian Lara, which is 400 not out.
